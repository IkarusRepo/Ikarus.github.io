\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This strategy adjusts the step size based on the ratio of target iterations to previous iterations.

The step size is changed according to the number of iterations of the previous step \[ \hat{s}_{k+1} = \sqrt{\frac{\hat{i}}{i_k}}\hat{s}_k,  \] where $\hat{s}_{k+1} $ is the new stesize and $\hat{i}$ is the desired number of iterations and $i_k$ is the number of iterations of the previous step.
