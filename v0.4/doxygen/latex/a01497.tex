Transform a Pre\+Basis into one with flat index-\/merging strategy

This utility takes a pre-\/basis and converts recursively all index-\/merging strategies into their flat analog, i.\+e. {\ttfamily Blocked\+Interleaved} is converted into {\ttfamily Flat\+Interleaved} and {\ttfamily Blocked\+Lexicographic} is transformed into {\ttfamily Flat\+Lexicographic}.
